System Architecture

From Raw News to Auditable
Risk Scores

A three-stage deterministic pipeline that collects, classifies, and scores — with full traceability at every step.

🗄️
1

Collect & Deduplicate

News collected from 5 authoritative sources with fund name matching. Cross-source deduplication retains the longest full-text version, with source priority ranking.

🤖
2

AI Classify

5-stage AI pipeline: Relevance → Nature → Category → Severity → Reasoning. Each event is tagged with a human-readable reasoning string for full auditability.

📊
3

Deterministic Scoring

Events scored using preset rules and category factors. A chronological risk timeline and waterline chart visualise score trends. 58 funds ranked by composite risk score.
